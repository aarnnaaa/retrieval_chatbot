Shipping Policy

1. Coverage:
   We deliver across all regions of India through trusted partners like Blue Dart, DTDC, and Delhivery.

2. Same-Day Dispatch:
   Orders placed before 5:00 PM IST on working days are dispatched the same day.

3. Delivery Timelines:
   - Metro cities: 1–2 business days
   - Tier 2/3 cities: 3–5 business days
   - Rural areas: 5–7 business days

4. Address Changes:
   Changes to shipping addresses can only be made before the order is shipped.

5. Shipping Fees:
   - Orders above ₹999 are eligible for free delivery.
   - Standard shipping: ₹49
   - Express shipping: ₹99
