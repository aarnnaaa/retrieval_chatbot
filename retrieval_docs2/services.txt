Available Services

1. Standard Delivery:
   Delivered within 3–5 business days across India. Available to all pincodes.

2. Express Delivery:
   Delivered within 1–2 business days in selected metro cities.

3. Same-Day Delivery:
   Currently available in Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Chennai.

4. Cash on Delivery (COD):
   Available for orders up to ₹5,000. A small COD fee of ₹30 applies.

5. International Shipping:
   Not supported currently. We ship only within India.

6. Weekend Delivery:
   Not available. Deliveries are made on business days (Mon–Fri).
