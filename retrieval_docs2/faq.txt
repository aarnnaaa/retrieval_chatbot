Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard delivery usually takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery can take 1–2 business days.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
A: Yes, you can update your address before the parcel is shipped. Once shipped, address changes are not possible.

Q: What happens if I miss my delivery?
A: If you miss the delivery attempt, the parcel is held at your nearest courier center for 3 days before being returned to the sender.

Q: Do I get a refund for canceled orders?
A: Yes. If your order is canceled before shipping, a full refund is initiated within 24 hours.

Q: What if my tracking link isn’t working?
A: Tracking updates may take up to 6 hours after the parcel is dispatched.
