Delivery & Tracking Updates

1. Tracking Activation:
   Tracking information becomes active within 6 hours after the parcel is dispatched from our warehouse.

2. Weather Delays:
   Due to heavy monsoon rains in North India, deliveries in Punjab, Haryana, and UP may be delayed by 1–2 days.

3. Festive Season Notice:
   Orders during Diwali, Christmas, and New Year may experience an additional delay of 2–3 business days due to high demand.

4. Courier Strikes:
   In the event of courier union strikes or transport disruptions, we will keep customers informed via email.

5. Dispatch Timings:
   Daily dispatch cut-off time is 5:00 PM IST.
